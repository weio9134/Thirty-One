INSTRUCTIONS

  • You start with a hand of 3 cards
  • When it's your turn, you may swap one of your cards with the cards in the middle
  • Your goal is to create the best possible hand against the other players
  • Highest possible hand value is 31 but good luck getting that
  • Only cards in the same suit may count towards your score
  • Aces are worth 11, face cards 10, and others are worth their face value
  • If you have 3 matching cards, your score is 30
  • When you believe you have a good hand, you may 'knock'
  • When you or others 'knock,' the other players still have one more chance to swap cards
  • Then, all hands are revealed. Lowest hand loses that round
  • 3 strikes and you're out
  • Good luck, have fun
